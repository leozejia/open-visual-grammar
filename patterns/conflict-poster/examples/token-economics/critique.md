# Critique

This example belongs to `conflict-poster` because the visual job is not calm
explanation. It needs a public feed first-read.

What matters:

- the conflict is real;
- the title or central metaphor carries the first read;
- details behave like texture;
- exact pricing tables are avoided because they would turn the cover into a
  brittle infographic.

The score can compile to an image prompt, but the prompt itself is not the
source of truth.
