# Case: Conflict Poster X Cover

## Job

Test whether a pattern can make one public argument visible in a scroll-heavy X
surface.

## Typical pattern

`patterns/conflict-poster/PATTERN.md`

## Scenario

A tutorial or essay has a sharp operational warning:

```text
The tool can do the thing, but the default path may cost more than users expect.
```

## Required score fields

- Viewer
- Job
- First read
- Tension
- Metaphor
- Composition
- Density
- Temperature
- Forbidden

## Runtime target

Image generation for X Article cover or X card cover.

## Three-card requirement

The three candidates should explore different conflict models:

- collision;
- meter or cost surface;
- fracture or compression.

Do not submit three minor style variants.

## Pass signals

- The argument is readable before opening the article.
- The visual has attitude without exaggerating the claim.
- The title and metaphor feel connected.
- The image still works when reduced to feed size.

## Fail signals

- Generic AI/software collage.
- Dense dashboard or infographic.
- Fake billing screenshot.
- Strong style but weak argument.
