# Case: Concept Explainer X Cover

## Job

Test whether a pattern can turn an abstract concept into a public, readable
hook.

## Typical pattern

`patterns/conflict-poster/PATTERN.md`

## Scenario

An article teaches a concept that sounds beneficial but has a hidden tradeoff:

```text
More context can be useful, but unmanaged context becomes cost and state risk.
```

## Required score fields

- Viewer
- Job
- First read
- Tension
- Metaphor distance
- Composition
- Density
- Temperature
- Forbidden

## Runtime target

Image generation for X Article cover or X single-image post.

## Three-card requirement

The three candidates should use different metaphor distances:

- near metaphor, such as ledger or workspace;
- medium metaphor, such as compression or overload;
- far metaphor, only if still legible.

## Pass signals

- The abstract idea becomes concrete without becoming childish.
- The first read survives downscaling.
- The metaphor does not require a paragraph of explanation.
- The visual is educational and still has tension.

## Fail signals

- Decorative abstract shapes.
- Overloaded small text.
- A metaphor so far that the concept disappears.
- Generic "AI brain" imagery.
