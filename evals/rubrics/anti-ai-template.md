# Rubric: Anti AI Template

This rubric catches images that look generated, generic, or style-first.

## Strong

- The image is built around a specific visual problem.
- The image contains a visible correction, not only a topic symbol.
- The metaphor is tailored to the article.
- The surface has intentional hierarchy and restraint.
- The output avoids stock AI symbols unless they are specifically justified.

## Weak

- Generic robot, glowing brain, abstract network, floating UI cards, or random
  futuristic panels.
- Style words dominate the prompt.
- The image looks plausible but says nothing.
- The image uses the same object-metaphor any AI post could use.
- The visual could be reused for any AI article without changing meaning.

## Operator questions

- Could this image fit ten unrelated AI posts?
- Is the style doing the work that the idea should do?
- Does any object in the image carry the article's specific conflict?
- What correction does the visual action make visible?
- Would I recognize this as our visual language without the title?
