# Conflict Poster Anti-Patterns

## Fake information design

Do not add charts, tables, terminal text, or dashboards unless the article is
actually about that surface.

## Decorative SaaS collage

Floating cards, random UI panels, fake analytics, and abstract gradients weaken
the conflict. They make the cover look generic.

## Too many claims

One poster cannot explain the whole article. If it contains three lessons, it
usually has no first read.

## Random English

Image models often invent labels. Avoid fake English UI text unless the output
will be cleaned in a deterministic editor.

## Overheated drama

Urgency is useful. False alarm is not.

If the article's argument is operational, keep the conflict operational. Do not
turn every cost lesson into a disaster scene.
