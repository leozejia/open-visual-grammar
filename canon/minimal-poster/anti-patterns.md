# Minimal Poster Anti-Patterns

## Empty premium

Whitespace without a strong idea is just absence.

## Decorative object

The object must carry the argument. It should not be a random symbol placed in
the middle of the page.

## Accidental brand ad

Avoid generic luxury shadows, glossy black boxes, and meaningless product-stage
lighting unless the subject is actually a product.

## Tiny unreadable title

Minimal does not mean illegible. If text is used, it must still be readable in
the target channel.
